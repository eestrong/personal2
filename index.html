Jazz on the Bridge

I have been a member of the Whitney M. Young Jazz Ensemble, in which I play the alto saxophone, since 2015. In that time I have played with the band at numerous events, including but not limited to Chicago Jazz Fest.

Learning Guitar

I began learning guitar when I took a "wiggle and strum" class with my little sister who is pictured to my right. After getting my bearings with the help of the class instructor I continued to teach myself guitar.
